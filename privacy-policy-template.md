# Privacy Policy Template

## Introduction
Military Fitness Hub is committed to protecting your privacy. This privacy policy explains how we collect, use, and disclose information when you use the app.

## Information we collect
- Profile data you enter, such as name, age, height, weight, and waist size.
- Fitness test data you log, including run times, plank time, pull-up count, MUF time, 880m run time, and ammo can lift count.
- App usage data stored locally in your device.

## How we use information
- To display your profile and fitness history within the app.
- To generate training and meal plan suggestions.
- To improve app features and performance.

## Data storage
- All user data is stored locally on the device using local storage.
- We do not transmit or store your personal fitness entries on external servers unless you explicitly implement an API integration.

## Third-party services
- The app uses no third-party analytics or tracking services by default.
- If AI integration is enabled in the future, a separate disclosure will describe the provider and how data is shared.

## Security
- We recommend using device-level security to protect your local app data.
- The app does not currently provide encryption beyond browser storage security.

## Changes to this policy
- We may update this privacy policy when app functionality changes.
- Users should review the policy periodically.

## Contact
For privacy questions, contact support@example.com.
